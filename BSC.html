BECHLOR OF SCIENCE

B.Sc full form is Bachelor of Science. The latin word for B.Sc is Baccalaureus Scientiae. B.Sc degree is like a foundation course for all the candidates who wish to pursue a career in the field of science. It is one of the most popular course choices among Science students after Class 12. Some of the popular B.Sc courses that students usually choose to pursue after Class 12 are B.Sc Physics, B.Sc Computer Science, B.Sc Chemistry, B.Sc Biology, B.Sc Mathematics among others.

Advantages of B.Sc

Scholarships- Government-funded learning opportunities or scholarships are offered to brilliant students who choose B.Sc courses. The benefits of these courses include beneficial offers such as covering all tuition costs that the student incurs while pursuing the course. Employment opportunities- The best part of doing a B.Sc degree is good job opportunities in the field of research and development. Strengthening the research and development industry in India is one of the main reasons why the government has provided more lucrative academics to B.Sc graduates. Exploring opportunities- BSc degrees have good job opportunities. Having said that, BSc students are not only restricted to science-related careers and have the ability to explore other fields such as administration, engineering, law, so on and so forth for both higher education and career opportunities. Types of B.Sc A bachelor's degree in science or B.Sc is of two types depending up on the mode of study Regular Correspondence A regular B.Sc degree is the one where candidates have to visit the college regularly and a certain minimum percentage of attendance is compulsory. However, students who canâ€™t go for the college on a regular basis due to some reasons can enroll for a B.Sc correspondence program where there is no boundation for attending the college. Students have to visit the college only at the time of practicals and theory examination. Also, depending on the the specialisation, a B.Sc degree is also divided into two major types namely B.Sc Honours B.Sc Pass/ B.Sc General A B.Sc honours degree is a program which develops advanced theoretical and research skills leading to specialization in one of the subjects studied during the entire degree course. The honours programs are taken by students who wish to pursue Master of Science (M.Sc) or Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) and a research or academic career. For example, B.Sc Botany, B.Sc Zoology, B.Sc Biotechnology etc. A B.Sc general or B.Sc pass or simply "B.Sc" is an academic degree designed to provide the students with foundation knowledge possible for a science-based career. For example, B.Sc Biology, B.Sc Physics, B.Sc Chemistry.
